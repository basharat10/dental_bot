# Professional Teeth Cleaning
Teeth cleaning involves removing plaque and tartar (mineralized plaque) that have accumulated on the teeth. 

## The Process
1. **Physical Exam:** A dental hygienist uses a small mirror to check around your teeth and gums for any signs of gingivitis (inflamed gums) or other potential concerns.
2. **Removing Plaque and Tartar:** Using a scaler, the hygienist removes plaque and tartar around your gum line, as well as in between your teeth.
3. **Gritty Toothpaste Cleaning:** Once your teeth are tartar-free, the hygienist brushes them with a high-powered electric brush.
4. **Expert Flossing:** Your hygienist can get deep between your teeth and locate any potential trouble spots.
5. **Rinsing:** Usually, you’ll get a rinse that contains liquid fluoride.

## Recovery & Benefits
- Prevents cavities and gum disease.
- Brightens your smile.
- No downtime; you can eat immediately after (unless fluoride treatment was applied).
