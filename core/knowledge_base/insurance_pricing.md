# Clinic Pricing & Insurance Guide

## Standard Procedure Pricing (Private Pay)
- **Consultation & X-Rays:** $150
- **Professional Cleaning (Adult):** $120
- **Professional Cleaning (Child):** $80
- **Simple Filling:** $150 - $250
- **Root Canal (Anterior):** $700 - $900
- **Root Canal (Molar):** $1,000 - $1,300
- **Dental Crown (Porcelain):** $1,200 - $1,500
- **Emergency Exam:** $95 (After-hours $150)

## Common Insurance Coverage (Estimates)
- **Preventative (Cleaning, Exams):** 100% Covered
- **Basic Procedures (Fillings, Extractions):** 80% Covered (Patient pays 20%)
- **Major Procedures (Crowns, Root Canals, Bridges):** 50% Covered (Patient pays 50%)

## Disclaimer
All prices are estimates. Final costs are determined after a clinical exam and insurance pre-authorization.
